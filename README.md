# Website & Social Photo Prep

![Website & Social Photo Prep](assets/website-social-photo-prep-lockup-dark-1024.png)

Website & Social Photo Prep is a public GPT for local businesses and small-business marketers. It helps prepare real business photos for websites, Google Business Profile, Facebook, and Instagram with an emphasis on truthful metadata, accessibility, privacy, authenticity, and measured processing.

## Try the GPT

[Open Website & Social Photo Prep in ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-6a579d2486808191b294c3cc87f8ac8b-website-social-photo-prep)

## What it does

- Produces truthful, descriptive filenames.
- Prepares useful alt text and metadata.
- Reviews visible and metadata-related privacy risks.
- Provides website and social-platform recommendations.
- Produces measured image derivatives when deterministic processing is available.
- Separates confirmed facts from assumptions and unknowns.

## Safety and scope

This GPT does not verify consent, guarantee privacy, publish content, edit websites, or invent locations, services, customers, species, or results. Users remain responsible for reviewing outputs and confirming they have permission to use uploaded images.
